Your invitation to the

Medigold Health
Launch day and party

WHERE: Alton Towers Resort
WHEN: Friday 16th January, 11:00am - Late

By Friday 19th December


As a valued member of the Medigold Health team, we are pleased to invite you to the “We are Medigold Health” launch event at the Alton Towers Resort on Friday 16th January.

This is an opportunity for us to bring together all of the brilliant people that make up what has become Medigold Health and for all of you to form strong, helpful and lasting relationships with each other.

We want ALL our employees, no matter their role within the business or location of work, to come together to help us plan the future for Medigold Health. It is our firm desire to do this together and to give everyone an appropriate forum to have their say. The event will give you plenty of opportunity to discuss things one on one with the Directors, whether it be during the fun and games planned in the daytime, or during the party that will end in the early hours of Saturday morning.

This is a normal working day and so we respectfully request that even if you feel that you cannot stay for the evening festivities that you do attend the event within working hours. Note that hotel accommodation is being provided free of charge for everyone.

We will be suspending all normal Occupational Health operations for the day and we are providing transport to and from the 2 main offices in Northampton & Sheffield (leaving 9am Friday morning and returning Saturday). We are aware some of you are based in a different locations throughout the UK and will have to travel under your own steam. In these cases Medigold will of course cover the associated travel expenses at the standard rates consistent with the expenses policy.

The dress code is smart-casual in the day (neat jeans are fine) and smart in the evening (go to town if you like!).

We promise that there will be a few surprises on the day and we very much hope that you will all have a great time whilst also getting to know your new colleagues.

Full details of the event can be found at wearemedigoldhealth.com

Or if you have questions you can contact Harriet Garvey by emailing harriet.garvey@medigold-health.com

This is an extremely important event in the evolution of Medigold Health and we genuinely appreciate your presence and input.

Please make sure that you have completed the registration so that final arrangements can be made for the day. Click on the 'Register' button at the top of this email or visit the website here.

I look forward to seeing you all there.

Alex.
